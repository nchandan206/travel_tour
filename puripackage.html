About Puri Tour Packages

On the map, Puri finds itself stamped in the state of Odisha in Eastern India and is fittingly called the ‘Temple Town’ due to its namesake, the Jagannath Temple, which is one of the foremost pilgrimage sites for Hindus, besides being known for having the largest kitchen in the world. Additionally, 24 festivals including 13 major ones are held every year in the temple premises. The ancient Markandeswar Shiva Temple and the Konark Sun Temple are one of the oldest monuments in India.\r\n\r\nPuri is blessed with numerous beaches such as Konark, Baleshwar, Swargadwar, Baliharachandi, et al. The Balukhand-Konark Wildlife Sanctuary boasts of sandy beaches, cashew plantations, coastal dunes and groves of Casuarina trees.

The Sudarshan Crafts Museum houses traditional sculptures, training and residential facilities, a Japanese-style Buddhist temple, library, showroom, and workshop. Chilika Wildlife Sanctuary is home to various migratory birds besides being an aquatic sanctuary to 225 plus species of fish. The sunrise and sunsets are sights to behold. Nalbana Bird Sanctuary or Nalbana Island is home to thousands of birds which descend during the migratory season.

5 things to do in Puri apart from visiting beaches


Relax at Model Beach

The perfect way to chill out during your vacation in Puri is to relax at the Model Beach. Visit the place anytime throughout the day and enjoy the serene beauty. Go on a long walk on the golden sand of the beach and relax on a beach chair. Let the sparkling waters of the sea lull you into tranquility.

Explore Chilika Wildlife senctuary

If you are a nature-lover, then Chilika Wildlife Sanctuary is the must-visit place near Puri. You can find a variety of exotic as well as migratory birds including heron, flamingo, brahminy kite and much more. Indulge in bird watching and take some captivating photographs. Along with the birds, you can spot some of the wildlife too.

Heritage Tours

Heritage tours are always unique and never to be missed by the travelers. Every heritage tour differs from another. In Puri, be a part of this interesting tour, which will take you to tribal areas, old Puri, Odiya cookery class and ‘Green Riders’ program. You can participate in all the activities at a pocket-friendly price.

Visit Konark Beach

Cool breeze sways around you while you take a casual walk on Konark Beach with your companions. Plan for a memorable vacation to this place and have unlimited fun and entertainment. Konark is famous for the Sun Temple, and it is located about 3 km away from the beach. The beach is quiet and you hardly get to see many tourists here. However, you can watch the fisherman busy with their daily chores.

Dolfin watching at Chilika

Chilika Lake is a must-visit place in Puri where you can watch the fishermen as they go about netting the catch of the day. The main attraction is to watch the dolphins playing and jumping in the lake. However, to spot the dolphins, you need to cover some distance from the shore. If you are lucky enough, then you can not only spot the dolphins but also capture them in your camera. Flamingos and other migratory birds can also be seen here.
