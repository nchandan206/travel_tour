4 things to do in kerela


Ziplining in Munnar

"Experience the thrill of gliding across the lush greens of Munnar with ziplining. Feel the rush as you take in the breathtaking views and enjoy the crisp mountain breeze This experience is sure to tickle your adventure bug.

Jeep Safari In Munnar

"Explore the beauty of Munnar atop an adorable elephant Enjoy the slow-paced adventure across rolling meadows and stunning mountains, taking in the lush sights. When in Kerala, this is certainly an experience worth having Note:Guests travelling to the city can avail this activity en- route"

Lakkam Waterfall Visit

Experience serenity in Munnar with a visit to Lakkam Waterfall. Make your way through the forest, along the rolling hills to reach the waterfall. Delight at the sight of the milky stream that flows down from a height of 50 feet.

Meesappulimalai Trek

Explore the outdoors in Kerala with a trek to Meesapulimala Peak. Start with a jeep ride to the starting point of the trek, breathing in the fresh air. Make your way through the winding trails to reach the top of the summit. A breathtaking view of tea estates and the Anayirangal dam awaits!
